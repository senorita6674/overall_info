Algorithm Pipeline
1
Input Sensor Fusion
Aggregate verified data from Instagram check-ins, Google popularity peaks, and qualitative reports across 25 venues in 8 geographic zones.
2
Noise Filtering
Discount closed venues (Mon closures, hours outside window) and apply capacity hard caps per venue.
3
Zone Count Estimation
Zone(t) = Σ venues × profile_intensity × peak_boost × pop_modifier, bounded by max capacity.
4
Density Calculation
Density = People ÷ Zone Area m². Thresholds: Low (<2), Moderate (2–5), High (5–10), Critical (>10 per 100m²).
5
Time-Pattern Matching
Cross-reference 24-pt hourly shape curves (weekday / weekend / festival) to validate and predict next-interval load.
6
Alert & Output
Flag zones at >80% capacity as CRITICAL. Recalculate flow rate from δ(count) between 30-min intervals.
